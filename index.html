Why this order works
Foundation first
Pain narratives only land if you know the product. Skipping straight to pains means you'll fumble follow-up questions about how Weel actually solves it.
Active recall > re-reading
Re-reading feels productive but doesn't stick. Pulling answers out — flashcards, quizzes, scenarios — is what builds real memory.
Spaced repetition
The flashcard deck weights itself. Cards you mark shaky surface 3× more often than ones you nail. Don't fight it.
Production > recognition
Recognising the right answer in a quiz is easy. Writing your own response in On The Phone is closer to a real call — that's the bar.

What the product offers

Six things, high level. Tap any tile for the detail.

💳
Smart Cards
+
Unlimited virtual & physical Visa cards, issued instantly. One per person, project, or team.
Virtual cards — created in seconds, not weeks. Apple Pay supported.
Physical cards — for staff that need to tap.
Pre-spend controls — spending policy lives on the card itself. Limits, merchant restrictions, time-of-day, locked categories. Anything outside policy is declined at the point of purchase.
Individual attribution — every transaction tied to a person. No more shared cards, no more mystery $340.
🧾
Smart Receipts
+
Receipts capture themselves at the moment of spend. Coded automatically. No more chasing.
In-app capture — staff snap a photo, app prompts them after every transaction.
OCR + GST extraction — merchant, amount, GST pulled automatically.
Auto-reminders — if a receipt is missing, Weel chases the staff member, not finance.
Auto-coding to GL — chart of accounts and tracking categories sync from Xero/MYOB/QBO/NetSuite. Rules engine + AI assigns the right code at the moment of spend, so staff don't pick from 200 cost centres.
✅
Approvals & Policies
+
Multi-level approval workflows. Real-time. Configurable by team, cost centre, dollar value or category.
Multi-level workflows — route by team, dollar threshold, cost centre, project.
Real-time notifications — approvers get pinged the second a transaction hits.
Full audit trail — every approval, decline, comment captured against the transaction.
Backups built in — no single point of failure when the GM is on leave.
📃
Accounts Payable
+
Bills, batch payments, vendor management. One AP workflow.
Bills in — via email, import from Xero, or manual entry.
AI OCR — pulls invoice data automatically.
Duplicate detection — flags duplicate invoices before they get paid twice.
Multi-level approvals — same approval engine as cards.
Batch payments — pay everything in one run.
💵
Reimbursements
+
Out-of-pocket spend handled in the same platform as cards. Not a bolt-on.
Submit in-app — staff snap a photo, fill in details, submit.
Approval & payout — manager approves, paid out direct.
One platform — cards and reimbursements together. No more two tools, no more reconciling between systems.
🔄
Subscriptions
+
Track recurring spend. Surface duplicates and creep. Cancel from inside Weel.
Auto-detection — recurring transactions flagged as subscriptions.
Price creep alerts — see when costs have crept up over time.
Cancel in-app — no chasing IT or the original signer.
← BackIntegrations

Weel syncs with the four accounting systems used by Australian mid-market.

Native, two-way, real-time. Setup is in clicks not weeks. Chart of accounts and tracking categories pull in automatically. Transactions push out the moment spend is approved — with receipts attached.

The four integrations
Xero
Most common
~70% of NDIS & family services orgs we walk into.
~70% of multi-site clinic groups we walk into.
In: Chart of accounts, tracking categories, GL codes — auto-imported.
Out: Transactions push to Xero the moment spend is approved, with receipt attached, ready to reconcile.
Hook: "If you're on Xero, this is plug-and-play. Most of our NDIS customers were live within a day."
Hook: "If you're on Xero, this is plug-and-play. Most of our multi-site clinic customers were live within a day."
MYOB AccountRight
Common
Established orgs, longer-tenured aged care & NDIS providers.
Established clinic groups, longer-tenured multi-site operators.
In: Chart of accounts, jobs, tax codes — auto-imported.
Out: Same model — transactions sync with receipts after approval.
Hook: "MYOB is fully supported. And if you're considering Xero down the track, we have a clean-cut migration path so you don't lose continuity."
QuickBooks Online
Less common AU
Smaller orgs, NZ exposure, occasionally global parent companies.
In/Out: Same two-way sync model.
Hook: "Yes, fully native — same experience as our Xero customers."
NetSuite
Enterprise tier
Larger / multi-entity orgs. Usually $50m+ revenue or multi-state operations.
In: GL codes, dimensions, departments, entities.
Out: Approved transactions flow into the right entity with clean GST coding. Multi-entity support built in.
Hook: "Sits in our Enterprise tier alongside SSO and API access. Common with our larger NDIS customers running multiple entities."
Hook: "Sits in our Enterprise tier alongside SSO and API access. Common with larger multi-site clinic groups — we've seen groups running anywhere from 3 to 16+ separate entities across locations, especially ones growing by acquisition."
What actually syncs
Two-Way
Chart of accounts, tracking categories, tax codes flow from the accounting system into Weel — so staff see clean dropdowns.
One-Way Out
Approved transactions, with receipts attached, push from Weel into the accounting system. Set export rules to control timing.
Status Tracking
Status column in Weel shows where every transaction sits between Weel and the accounting system. Nothing falls through.
Setup Time
Connect in a few clicks. Most orgs are syncing the same day. No IT project, no implementation phase.
